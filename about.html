Problem Statement

The overuse of Thermocol (Expanded Polystyrene - EPS) poses a serious threat to the environment due to its non-biodegradable nature. Our goal is to replace it with an eco-friendly alternative.

Mission

To develop and promote eco-friendly packaging made from mushroom-based mycelium, adhering to environmental standards and minimizing ecological impact.
