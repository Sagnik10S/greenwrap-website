About Us

GreenWrap is a student-led startup project from Techno India University, Kolkata. We aim to replace traditional Thermocol packaging with biodegradable, sustainable alternatives using mushroom mycelium and agricultural waste.

Our interdisciplinary team brings together technical, creative, and business minds to create solutions that are innovative, practical, and eco-friendly.

Our Mission

To develop scalable biodegradable packaging solutions that reduce environmental damage and offer viable alternatives to plastic and thermocol in modern industries.
