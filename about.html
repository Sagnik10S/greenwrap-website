About Us

GreenWrap is a student-led startup project from Techno India University, Kolkata. We aim to replace traditional Thermocol packaging with biodegradable, sustainable alternatives using mushroom mycelium and agricultural waste.

Inspired by the need for eco-conscious packaging in industries like cosmetics, consumer electronics, and foodware, we are building prototypes that are lightweight, compostable, and market-ready â€” reducing waste while protecting products effectively.

Our Mission

To create scalable, biodegradable packaging solutions that promote a cleaner environment while being cost-effective and durable enough for commercial use.
