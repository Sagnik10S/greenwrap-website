About Us

GreenWrap is a student-led initiative from Techno India University, aiming to revolutionize the packaging industry with sustainable, biodegradable solutions. Our primary product is a mycelium-based packaging alternative to traditional Thermocol (EPS).

With environmental concerns rising and plastic pollution at its peak, we’re taking a bold step forward by using agricultural waste and fungal mycelium to grow eco-friendly packaging materials that are fully compostable and naturally sourced.

Our Mission

To innovate biodegradable packaging solutions that reduce ecological impact while providing efficient protection for consumer goods.
