Business Model & Financial Viability

We’ve structured our model to ensure scalable, impactful growth by aligning with sustainability goals across industries.

Revenue Streams

  • B2B Sales: Supplying directly to cosmetics, electronics, FMCG, and export companies
  • Partnerships: Licensing and co-production with existing packaging manufacturers
  • Premium Customization: Eco-packaging for high-end branding requirements
  • Grants & Subsidies: Government incentives supporting green technologies

Market Feasibility

  • Matches paper packaging pricing today
  • Expected to match Thermocol prices within 3–4 years
  • EBITDA projected at 30–40% with long-term contracts

Challenges

  • Current production costs slightly higher than plastics
  • Durability in wet conditions requires improvement
  • Scaling up production and logistics for mass adoption

Future Scope

  • Improving water resistance and mechanical strength
  • Government collaboration to accelerate adoption
  • Expansion to global markets – USA, EU, Southeast Asia

Our Stakeholders

  • Suppliers: Farmers, agri-waste providers
  • Manufacturers: Mold and processing partners
  • Customers: Companies seeking sustainable alternatives
  • Funders: VCs, government, sustainability grants
  • Regulators: Environmental agencies, compliance boards
  • End Users: Individuals and eco-conscious businesses
